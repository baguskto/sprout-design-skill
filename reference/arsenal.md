# Creative Arsenal

Curated pattern catalog. Pull from here when a verb (typically `craft`, `delight`, `overdrive`) needs a non-generic composition. Reference, not copy-paste.

**Sprout rule:** NEVER ship a stock pattern unmodified. Adapt at least 1–2 properties (color, timing, shape, easing, or composition) to the project context. The patterns below are starting points, not deliverables.

## Heroes

- **Asymmetric Split** — Text cleanly aligned left (or right); high-quality relevant image fills the opposite half with a subtle fade into the section background. Default substitute for the banned centered hero.
- **Full-Bleed Type Mask** — Massive display type acts as a transparent window onto a video or image background. Use when stopping power matters more than scannability.
- **Editorial Pull-Quote Hero** — Magazine-style: massive serif quote, byline below, asset on a hard grid. Right for luxury / editorial flavors.
- **Kinetic Marquee Hero** — Endless horizontal text band as the headline, slower band beneath, content blocks below. Right for brand-forward landing pages.
- **Bento Hero** — Hero IS the first Bento grid: 1 tile holds the headline + CTA, surrounding tiles preview features. Right for product-led growth pages.

## Navigation

- **Mac OS Dock Magnification** — Edge-anchored nav; icons scale fluidly on hover. Use sparingly — high motion, high attention cost.
- **Magnetic Button** — Buttons physically pull toward the cursor via `useMotionValue`. For primary CTAs only when motion ≥ 6.
- **Gooey Menu** — Sub-items detach from the main button like viscous liquid. Right for playful / experimental brands.
- **Dynamic Island** — Pill that morphs to show status, alerts, or active task. Right for utility apps and AI-assistant surfaces.
- **Contextual Radial Menu** — Circular menu expanding at the click coordinates. Right for canvas / editor surfaces.
- **Floating Speed Dial (FAB)** — Springs into a curved line of secondary actions. Right for mobile-first surfaces.
- **Mega Menu Reveal** — Full-screen dropdown stagger-fades complex content. Right for marketing sites with deep IA.

## Layouts

- **Bento Grid** — Asymmetric, tile-based grouping. Apple Control Center reference. The Sprout default for feature sections at variance ≥ 5.
- **Masonry Layout** — Staggered, no fixed row heights. Right for gallery / portfolio.
- **Chroma Grid** — Grid borders or tiles show subtle, continuously animating gradients. Right for AI-product surfaces (motion ≥ 6).
- **Split Screen Scroll** — Two halves slide opposite on scroll. Right for product comparison or story scrolls.
- **Curtain Reveal** — Hero parts in the middle like a curtain on scroll. Right for high-drama brand launches.

## Cards

- **Parallax Tilt Card** — 3D tilt tracking mouse coordinates. Use on hero cards only; tilt is attention-expensive.
- **Spotlight Border Card** — Card borders illuminate under cursor. Right for dark-mode feature grids.
- **Glassmorphism Panel** — True frosted glass with inner refraction border. Only when there is real content behind it (banned as decoration).
- **Holographic Foil Card** — Iridescent rainbow shifts on hover. Right for membership / premium tiers.
- **Tinder Swipe Stack** — Physical stack swipeable away. Right for content-discovery surfaces.
- **Morphing Modal** — Button seamlessly expands into a full-screen dialog. Replacement for the standard modal pattern.

## Scroll

- **Sticky Scroll Stack** — Cards stick to top and physically stack. Right for storytelling sections.
- **Horizontal Scroll Hijack** — Vertical scroll → horizontal gallery pan. Right for portfolios / case studies.
- **Locomotive Scroll Sequence** — Video/3D framerate tied to scrollbar. Apple-style product reveal. Heavy — variance ≥ 8 only.
- **Zoom Parallax** — Background image zooms continuously on scroll. Right for atmospheric brand pages.
- **Scroll Progress Path** — SVG line draws itself as user scrolls. Right for step-by-step walkthroughs.
- **Liquid Swipe Transition** — Page transitions wipe like viscous liquid. Right for narrative microsites.

## Galleries

- **Dome Gallery** — 3D panoramic dome composition. Right for immersive portfolio pages.
- **Coverflow Carousel** — 3D carousel, center focused, edges angled back. Right for media-rich navigation.
- **Drag-to-Pan Grid** — Boundless grid the user drags in any direction. Right for canvas / mood-board UX.
- **Accordion Image Slider** — Narrow strips expand fully on hover. Right for case-study galleries.
- **Hover Image Trail** — Mouse leaves a trail of popping/fading images. Right for designer / studio portfolios.
- **Glitch Effect Image** — Brief RGB-channel shift on hover. Right for music / culture / underground brands.

## Typography

- **Kinetic Marquee** — Endless text band; reverses or speeds up on scroll. Cheap and effective for brand identity.
- **Text Mask Reveal** — Massive type as transparent window over video. Pair with full-bleed hero.
- **Text Scramble Effect** — Matrix-style decoding on load or hover. Right for AI / cyber flavors.
- **Circular Text Path** — Text curved along a spinning circle. Right for brand seals and logo decoration.
- **Gradient Stroke Animation** — Outlined text, gradient runs along the stroke. Reserve for annotation-scale type, never display.
- **Kinetic Typography Grid** — Letters in a grid dodge or rotate away from the cursor. Right for type-led brand pages.

## Micro-interactions

- **Particle Explosion Button** — CTA shatters into particles on success. Right for celebration moments (signup complete, payment success).
- **Liquid Pull-to-Refresh** — Mobile reload indicator as detaching water droplets. Right for native-feel mobile web.
- **Skeleton Shimmer** — Shifting light reflection across placeholder boxes. Mandatory loading state at motion ≥ 4.
- **Directional Hover-Aware Button** — Hover fill enters from the side the mouse came from. Adds dimension to flat buttons.
- **Ripple Click Effect** — Visual waves from click coordinates. Right for Material-adjacent surfaces.
- **Animated SVG Line Drawing** — Vectors draw their own contours in real-time. Right for diagrams and process steps.
- **Mesh Gradient Background** — Organic lava-lamp color blobs. Use as backdrop only, never on foreground type.
- **Lens Blur Depth** — Dynamic background-layer blur to highlight a foreground action. Right for focus-mode / command-palette overlays.
